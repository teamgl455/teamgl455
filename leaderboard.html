CityLondon Annual Gala, Royal Danish Embassy (30/09/22)

Fundraising objectives for Ndururumo High School, Kenya:

  • 1. Teaching supplies (DKK 10,000)
  • 2. Classroom equipment for 5 classrooms (DKK 50,000)
  • 3. 1 state-of-the-art science laboratory (DKK 100,000)
  • 4. Dormatories with beds for 150 students (DKK 500,000)
  • 5. A complete reconstruction of the main school building (DKK 1,000,000)
